My Project entitled “AESTHETICS WORLD” provides a platform for people who work behind the cultural artform of Kerala. Through this system I also provide an opportunity to foreigners to know about the culture of Kerala. “AESTHETHISC WORLD” system is a JSP project that provide a platform for people who work behind on Kerala artform, unemployed people and also for aesthetics. Here the users can search different freelancers as well as packages. Through the package it means that the users are provided with cultural artforms with an efficient guide. The payment is done by online. Users can also book the freelancers to conduct cultural programs. This will be an open website and everybody can access to view the details after registration. .
